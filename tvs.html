Common TV Issues We Fix

We diagnose the root cause of power failures, backlight issues, and signal loss for all major brands in Viera West, Viera East, and Rockledge.

  • Screens go black but sound works: Usually a backlight failure or power board issue.
  • No power / No lights: Power supply board or main board failure.
  • HDMI port not recognized: Physical port replacement or signal trace repair.
  • Vertical lines or flickering: T-Con board or panel driver issues.
