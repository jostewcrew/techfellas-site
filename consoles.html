Gaming Console Repair in Viera & Rockledge, FL

What We Fix

Consoles are power-dense machines. We handle the heat, the shorts, and the "ghosts" in the machine for clients in Viera West, Viera East, and Rockledge.

  • HDMI Port Repair: "No Signal" issues, loose ports, and port replacement for Xbox Series X/S, PS5, and Switch.
  • Overheating Solutions: Thermal paste replacement, fan cleaning, and heatsink reseating to stop shutdowns.
  • Boot Loop & Dead Console: Diagnosing and fixing "Red Ring of Death," "Blue Light of Death," and power board shorts.
  • Board-Level Component Replacement: Replacing failed capacitors, power ICs, and HDMI controllers.
  • Audio/Video Issues: Fixing distorted sound or video artifacts.
